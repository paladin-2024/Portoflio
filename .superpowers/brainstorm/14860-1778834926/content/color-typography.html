Typography Upgrade (applies to all options)

Keep Syne for headings (it's great). Replace DM Sans with Plus Jakarta Sans — sharper, more professional, better weight range. JetBrains Mono stays for code/terminal elements.

PLUS JAKARTA SANS 400/500 — Body text, descriptions
JETBRAINS MONO — Labels, badges, terminal
